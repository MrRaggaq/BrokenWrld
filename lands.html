Lands

How does it work

There are 3 types of lands of different rarity. They differ in the number of people who can restore the land and the speed of mining the NON token. To craft land you need to collect 4 identical elements and glass. Land owners will receive a percentage of people who stake their tools on their territory. A pack with materials for crafting lands will be available soon.
